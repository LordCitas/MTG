A Saga of Myth and Battle

Kaldheim is a legendary plane inspired by Norse mythology and steeped in tales of heroism, gods, and eternal conflict. This plane is a tapestry of ten interconnected realms, each with its own distinct landscape, culture, and inhabitants. From the icy expanses of Istfell to the verdant, elfin forests of Skemfar, Kaldheim is a world where myth and reality collide.

At the heart of Kaldheim’s identity are its gods, powerful beings who shape the plane and interact directly with its people. These deities are not distant figures but active participants in the sagas that define Kaldheim’s history. Mortals, too, play a crucial role, living lives full of daring exploits that are immortalized in the storytelling traditions of the plane.

The plane is in constant flux, its realms shifting and clashing through a phenomenon known as the Doomskar, a cosmic event that sparks chaos and opens paths between worlds. This volatility fuels Kaldheim’s culture of warbands, raids, and epic quests, where bravery and strength are paramount. Every action could inspire a saga and every battle could determine the fate of the realm.

Istfell

Istfell, in the realm of Kaldheim, is a mysterious and ethereal plane that serves as the resting place of spirits and the land of the dead. Shrouded in mist and illuminated by a cold, ghostly light, it is a place where the echoes of ancient warriors, forgotten lore, and lost souls linger.
Governed by a solemn peace, Istfell embodies both reflection and the inevitability of fate. It is a realm of quiet power, where the living tread carefully to seek wisdom from the departed.

“To the living, the horn sounds faint and mournful, but to the spirits of Istfell, it is a thunderous call that must be obeyed.”

Starnheim

Starnheim is a radiant and majestic plane, the glorious afterlife for the most honorable and valorous souls. It is ruled by the Valkyries, angelic beings who guide the worthy dead to their eternal reward.
Bathed in golden light and towering celestial halls, Starnheim embodies ideals of heroism, justice, and eternal glory. It is a realm of endless feasting and battle, where the honored dead prepare for the final confrontation in the eternal cycle of destiny.

“If we fall today, let us fall with honor, defending our realm from the horrors that would defile it. Forward, shieldmates! To Starnheim!”

Axgard

Axgard is the fiery and industrious realm of the dwarves, a land of rugged mountains, roaring forges, and unbreakable craftsmanship. Known for their unmatched skill in metalwork and invention, the dwarves of Axgard forge legendary weapons and artifacts, blending tradition with innovation.
The realm is characterized by its volcanic terrain and bustling halls, where community and creativity thrive. Axgard embodies resilience, ingenuity, and the enduring spirit of those who shape their destiny with hammer and flame.

"The dwarves of Axgard seldom have reason to leave their impregnable underground kingdom—but when they do, they go in force."

Bretagard

Bretagard is a vast and fertile realm of mortals, embodying the spirit of resilience and community. Its rolling plains, dense forests, and rugged coastlines are home to clans of humans and elves who live in harmony with nature, defending their lands from constant threats.
Divided into villages and strongholds, Bretagard is steeped in traditions of honor, survival, and respect for the divine. It is a land of heroes and hearths, where mortals carve out their destinies against the odds.

“When the path to Starnheim was opened, vengeance rained down upon the enemies of Bretagard.”

Karfell

Karfell is a grim and icy realm ruled by the draugr, undead warriors bound by loyalty to their king and their unyielding purpose. Its frozen fjords, crumbling fortresses, and eerie silence speak of a once-great kingdom now steeped in death and decay.
The draugr of Karfell, though lifeless, retain their cunning and martial prowess, serving as relentless guardians of their frozen home. Karfell embodies cold determination and the haunting echoes of a proud past that refuses to fade.

“Locked in the ice of Karfell, the treasures of ancient nobles await in ruins guarded by the dead.”

Surtland

Surtland is a land of fire and chaos, home to the mighty giants who embody raw power and destruction. Towering volcanoes, rivers of molten lava, and jagged peaks dominate the landscape, mirroring the fiery temperament of its inhabitants.
The giants of Surtland, driven by ancient rivalries and primal fury, revel in battle and conquest, leaving scorched earth in their wake. This realm represents untamed might and the relentless forces of nature, thriving in a world forged by flame and fury.

“What fire giants lack in arcane prowess, they make up for with reckless, explosive rage.”

Littjara

Littjara is the mystical and ever-shifting realm of the shapeshifters, a land where identity and form are fluid. Its landscapes mirror this mutability, with forests, lakes, and skies constantly shifting in color and shape.
The changelings of Littjara blend seamlessly into their surroundings, embodying adaptability and the mysteries of transformation. This realm thrives on trickery and ambiguity, where nothing is as it seems, reflecting the boundless creativity and enigmatic nature of its shapeshifting inhabitants.

“To escape Littjara, follow a bird.”

—Tuskeri folklore

Immersturm

Immersturm is a tempestuous and war-torn realm, home to demons and berserkers who thrive in its endless chaos. A land of fiery storms, jagged mountains, and blood-soaked battlefields, it is a place where conflict is eternal and peace is unknown.
The inhabitants, driven by unrelenting fury and ambition, embody the wild, destructive energy of this realm. Immersturm is a crucible of rage and power, where only the strongest survive, forged by fire and bound by the storms of endless war.

“Panic spread like wildfire as the demons of Immersturm poured into Bretagard, slaughtering as they came.”

Skemfar

Skemfar is the ancient and shadowed realm of the elves, steeped in history and tradition. Its dense forests, towering trees, and hidden groves are sacred grounds, echoing with the wisdom of ages past. Divided into the Wood and Shadow clans, the elves of Skemfar embody a delicate balance between growth and decay, life and death.
Fiercely protective of their ancestral lands, they are masters of both nature’s vitality and its more somber, inevitable cycles. Skemfar is a realm of pride, reverence, and enduring strength.

“Stripped of their divinity, the elves of Skemfar channeled their rage and sorrow into bloody brutality.”

Gnottvold

Gnottvold is the rugged and untamed forested realm of the trolls, a place of ancient trees, moss-covered stones, and primal strength. Shrouded in mist and mystery, it is a land where nature’s raw power reigns supreme.
The trolls of Gnottvold are formidable beings, embodying resilience and ferocity, with a deep connection to their wild and unyielding home. This realm thrives on the interplay of brutality and survival, where the ancient woods guard secrets as enduring as the trolls themselves.

“Tread carefully! The last time I walked this path, half the snowdrifts were, in fact, sleeping trolls. I’ve never run so fast in my life!”

—Iskene, Kannah storyteller
