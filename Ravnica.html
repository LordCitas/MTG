El plano de los dioses y semidioses

Los habitantes de Theros trabajan duro para ganarse el pan y el agua de cada día, pero nada de eso les impide rezar y hacer ofrendas a sus dioses patrones. Entidades divinas y etéreas, los dioses supervisan el funcionamiento de Theros y los elementos, cada uno movido por una ideología propia:

Previous plane: Phyrexia

Following plain: Bloomburrow

Azorius Senate

The Azorius Senate is a guild dedicated to law, order, and structured governance, striving to maintain control and stability in all aspects of society. Led by the sphinx Isperia, they draft and enforce intricate laws to ensure a tightly regulated world, and they excel at delay tactics, control strategies, and neutralizing threats, using rules and bureaucracy as both weapons and shields to preserve authority.
Their colors, white and blue, symbolize their ideals of justice and logic.

“The rules of logic and order have already made the choice for you.”

—Isperia

Orzhov Syndicate

The Orzhov Syndicate is a guild of wealth, power, and spiritual control, blending religion with ruthless business practices. Led by the Ghost Council, they thrive on exploiting debts, contracts, and loyalty to maintain their grip on the living and the dead, often focusing on life manipulation, extortion, and the relentless accrual of resources in order to ensure their dominion is absolute and eternal.
Their colors, white and black, represent a balance between structured authority and self-serving ambition.

“If you think the cathedral is impressive, you should see what’s down below.”

—Teysa

Boros Legion

The Boros Legion represents justice, zeal, and military might. Known for their unwavering commitment to order, they blend martial discipline with fiery passion. Guided by the angel Aurelia, they embody both the righteous ideals of protecting the weak and the fervor of punishing transgressors, focusing on swift, aggressive tactics and synergy, emphasizing teamwork and overwhelming force in battle.
Their colors, red and white, symbolize this duality of passion and structure.

“There is no time to remedy our enemies’ ignorance. Blast it out of them.”

—Aurelia

Selesnya Conclave

The Selesnya Conclave embodies harmony, community, and a deep connection to nature. Guided by the ancient dryad Trostani, they seek to unite all life under a shared vision of peace and growth. Selesnya thrives on synergy, summoning vast armies of creatures and strengthening their ranks with cooperation and unity.
Their colors, green and white, represent natural flourishing and collective order, and so, they view individuality as secondary to the greater good, striving for balance in all things.

“In each of us is the strength of all of us.”

—Trostani

House Dimir

House Dimir is a secretive and manipulative guild, thriving in shadows and secrecy. Known as Ravnica's spies and schemers, they excel in espionage, deception, and uncovering hidden truths. Guided by the elusive Lazav, their motives are shrouded in mystery, their strategies revolving around subterfuge, milling opponents' resources, and wielding precise, covert strikes to maintain control.
Their colors, blue and black, reflect their mastery of intellect and ambition, combined with a willingness to use underhanded tactics.

“Who are the agents of the Dimir? No one and everyone.”

—Lazav

Izzet League

The Izzet League is a guild of innovation, experimentation, and chaos, driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge. Led by the mage Ral Zarek, a young prodigy, the Izzet constantly push the boundaries of science and magic. Known for their reckless experiments and brilliant inventions, they thrive on unpredictability.
Their colors, blue and red, embody intellect and passion, combining creativity with explosive energy, their strategies often focusing on spellslinging, combo potential, and unpredictable bursts of power.

“Good ideas don’t take time. They take a lot of bad ideas first.”

—Ral Zarek

Simic Combine

The Simic Combine is a guild devoted to growth, evolution, and the seamless fusion of magic and biology. Guided by a focus on adapting life to overcome challenges, they shape creatures and ecosystems to achieve perfection. They excel at creating powerful hybrid creatures and enhancing their forces through mutation and strive for balance between nature and progress, blending organic life with magical innovation.
Their colors, green and blue, symbolize natural growth and intellectual curiosity.

“To survive exceptional times we must adapt by exceptional means.”

—Vannifar

Culto Rakdos

The Rakdos Cult thrives on chaos, hedonism, and destruction, blending dark humor with brutality. Led by the demon Rakdos himself, they revel in anarchy and theatrical carnage, seeing mayhem as both art and entertainment. They also embody wild aggression and unpredictable power, with strategies that overwhelm opponents through relentless attacks, sacrifice, and devastating effects.
Their colors, black and red, reflect their passion for freedom and unrestrained ambition, often at the cost of others.

“Let all feel joy in pain.”

—Rakdos

Golgari Swarm

The Golgari Swarm is a guild that embodies life, death, and decay, finding strength in the natural cycle of renewal. Led by the gorgon Vraska, they thrive in the shadows, embracing rot and growth as essential forces. Golgari strategies focus on reanimation, resource recursion, and overwhelming opponents with swarms of creatures, ensuring nothing is wasted in their relentless cycle of life.
Their colors, black and green, symbolize death’s inevitability and nature’s resilience.

“Shut the gates. In the Underrealm we will outlast the coming doom.”

—Vraska

Gruul Clans

The Gruul Clans are a wild and untamed guild, rejecting civilization and embracing primal freedom. They consist of warriors, shamans, and outcasts united by their disdain for the structured order of society. Led by the cyclops Borborygmos, they revere nature's raw power and seek to reclaim Ravnica's wilds. Gruul strategies emphasize aggressive, overwhelming force and destruction, embodying their chaotic desire to tear down the civilized world.
Their colors, red and green, symbolize fiery passion and natural strength.

“Not Gruul? Then die!”

—Borborygmos
