A Compleated Hivemind

New Phyrexia is a dark and twisted plane known for its grim and dystopian nature. It is a world dominated by the Phyrexians, a race of horrific, biomechanical creatures bent on spreading their twisted version of perfection across the multiverse. Originally a plane known as Mirrodin, New Phyrexia was overtaken and corrupted by the Phyrexian oil, transforming the entire world into a nightmarish landscape of metal, flesh, and disease.

The Phyrexians, led by their twisted Mother of Machines, Elesh Norn, aim to conquer and 'compleat' all other lifeforms, merging them with their cruel and unfeeling vision of perfection. The plane is divided into five distinct "wells", each governed by a different faction of Phyrexian leadership. These factions, each representing different aspects of Phyrexian ideology, are in constant conflict, vying for control of the plane and, eventually, the multiverse.

The landscape of New Phyrexia is a surreal blend of metal and organic matter, with landscapes of twisted structures, oil-slicked rivers, and mechanical monstrosities. The plane is a place of perpetual suffering, where life is forcibly altered into something unnatural and grotesque. However, remnants of the old Mirrodin still linger, with pockets of resistance formed by surviving planeswalkers and corrupted beings fighting against the Phyrexian onslaught.

Elesh Norn

Leader of the Phyrexians, Elesh Norn embodies the concept of perfection through compleation, a process of transforming beings into Phyrexian machines. With a commanding presence, her appearance combines organic and mechanical elements, including a bone mask and intricate armor.
Ruthless and strategic, she seeks to impose her vision of Phyrexian dominance across the multiverse, using manipulation and force to expand her influence and reshape reality in her image.

“Abandon your selfish ambitions and become one with the Flesh Singularity.”

—Elesh Norn

Jin-Gitaxias

Known for his pursuit of scientific perfection and experimentation, Jin-Gitaxias has a brilliant and twisted mind, as he seeks to advance Phyrexian technology and biological manipulation through grotesque experiments. His appearance is a fusion of machine and flesh, with a cold, calculating presence.
Jin-Gitaxias is driven by a desire to reshape life through his vision of compleation, believing that only through perfecting the body and mind can true power be achieved.

“I despise Vorinclex and his slobberings about ‘evolution’. Only I know true progress.”

—Jin-Gitaxias, Core Augur

Sheoldred

Sheoldred is one of the many black-aligned thanes embodying the dark aspects of ambition and control, and as the leader of the Phyrexian Swamp, she thrives on manipulation and domination. Her appearance is a horrifying fusion of organic and mechanical parts, with a towering, insect-like form.
Sheoldred's cunning and ruthlessness make her a master of deceit, using her influence to subjugate others. Her ultimate goal is to spread Phyrexian compleation, transforming all life into obedient servants of her vision.

“Leave no survivors. Let the other worlds see what befalls those who attempt to resist the might of Phyrexia.”

—Sheoldred

Urabrask

The red Praetor Urabrask is known for his unconventional and rebellious nature. Unlike his fellow Phyrexians, he resists the complete tyranny of the other Praetors, favoring a form of chaotic independence. Urabrask’s appearance blends mechanical and organic features, with fiery, molten elements symbolizing his connection to the Red Phyrexian ideals.
He values freedom and disruption, preferring to let his followers act according to their own will, while still advancing the Phyrexian agenda in his own way.

“It’s a beautiful vision. Discarded debris is reborn as a singular entity.”

—Urabrask the Hidden

Vorinclex

Vorinclex is the green Praetor representing the savage and brutal aspects of nature. He embodies strength, predation, and the ruthless survival of the fittest. With a massive, monstrous form resembling a blend of predator and machine, his presence is terrifying.
Vorinclex rules over the Phyrexian forest with an iron fist, seeking to dominate and consume all in his path. His ultimate goal is to reshape the natural world into a harsh, brutal Phyrexian environment where only the strongest endure.

“Dead or alive, my creations are stronger than Jin-Gitaxias’s septic minions.”

—Vorinclex, Voice of Hunger
